About Me

Cloud Infrastructure Product Leader driving $1B+ in revenue with transformative solutions at Equinix, IBM, and Huawei that drive enterprise digital transformation.

With a proven track record at Equinix and IBM, I've architected enterprise networking solutions, launched disruptive products, and led global teams across 3 continents. My expertise spans hybrid and multi-cloud networking and architectures, AI-driven product strategy, and enterprise sales—delivering measurable business impact at scale.

Over 37 years, I've learned that great products start with deep customer understanding. My journey began in 1988 providing field technical support for banking systems—learning how mission-critical infrastructure must perform under pressure. That experience shaped how I approach every product decision: from designing IBM ServicePac (the company's first bundled services offering) to launching Equinix's Fabric Cloud Router ($1M+ ARR in year one, 22% monthly growth). I've led teams through transformational change—implementing new product development methodologies at Huawei across US, Europe, and China operations, and guiding Equinix's strategic shift from infrastructure company to software-native platform.

Currently driving Cloud Networking innovation at Equinix as Principal Product Manager and recently completed an AI product development certification from MIT to shape next-generation cloud services.

What Drives Me

Customer obsession: Every product starts with deep customer understanding. Team building: Mentored dozens of product managers who progressed to leadership roles. Strategic thinking: Positioned organizations early for emerging opportunities. Execution excellence: Consistent delivery of growth targets at scale.
